The Creators

The minds behind the digital architecture of the 2040 education system.

Aman Adlakha

Lead Developer

I am Aman Adlakha. I am the lead developer of this project.

I built the digital structure of this website from scratch. I used my skills to create a futuristic dark colored theme for the website.

I used CSS, HTML, and JavaScript to create it. I used Google Fonts for the modern text and Font-Awesome to add all the glowing icons you see.

Vikrant Choudhary

Content Writer

I am Vikrant Choudhary. I am the content writer and researcher for our project.

I imagined what a school in the year 2040 would actually look like. I thought deeply about how Artificial Intelligence and Virtual Reality could change the way we study in classrooms. I gathered all the ideas about robots and holograms.

I wrote all the text you are reading on this website. I made sure to explain the science and technology in simple words. My goal was to make sure that everyone can easily understand our ideas for the future.
