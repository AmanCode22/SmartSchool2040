Welcome To The Future

The year is 2040. Schools are no longer boring buildings with old wooden desks and heavy blackboards. The future of education is bright, digital, and completely magical.

A New Era of Education

We imagined a world where going to school is the most exciting part of a student's day. In our vision of Smart School 2040, heavy school bags full of paper books are a thing of the past.

Every student gets a thin, unbreakable glass screen. This single glass screen holds all the knowledge of the entire world. It connects directly to the school computer brain and updates automatically.

AI Companions

Artificial Intelligence is now a student's best friend. There is no need to wait for a human teacher to become free to ask a difficult question.

Smart robots stand in the corners of every room. If you do not understand a complex math problem, you just have to speak out loud. The robot will explain the topic to you in simple words immediately.

Global Connection

We built this project to share our ideas about how wonderful learning can become. When technology is used the right way, no student is left behind.

We believe that technology will remove all borders. This allows students in India to sit in the same digital classroom as students in Japan.
