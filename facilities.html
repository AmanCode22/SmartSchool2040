Campus Facilities

The physical spaces designed to hold the digital world of tomorrow.

The Main Data Center

The most important room in the entire school is the Main Data Center. This is a huge, cold room filled with blinking lights, thick cables, and massive super computers.

It acts as the physical brain of the campus. It controls all the teaching robots, stores all the student marks safely, and runs the complex Artificial Intelligence software.

The Robot Garage

The school has a special room called the Robot Garage. Here, students work with their real hands instead of sitting in front of screens.

They are given broken robot parts and tools. The teachers teach them how to put the motors and wires together to build real machines that can walk and talk.

Hologram Rooms

A Hologram Room is a dark and empty room. When the teacher presses a button, special laser lights shoot down from the ceiling.

These lasers draw a 3D picture of a human heart or a car engine floating right in the middle of the room. Students can walk all the way around the glowing object to see how it works.

3D Printing Labs

When students design something amazing on their smart glass screens, they do not just look at it. They can send their design directly to the 3D Printing Lab.

This room has magical machines that melt hot plastic and build real objects layer by layer. If a student draws a new type of bridge on the computer, the machine will print a real, hard plastic copy of that design for them to hold in their hands.

Kinetic Sports Arena

Physical education is now more productive. The gym floors are made of special tiles that collect energy when students run and play.

This energy is stored in the school's batteries to help power the computers. It teaches us how our own physical movement can create clean power for the world.

Smart Canteen

The school canteen does not sell junk food. When a student walks in, a machine quickly scans their body to see what vitamins they need today.

The robot chef then prepares a custom, healthy meal that tastes exactly like their favorite food but is made entirely of healthy ingredients.
